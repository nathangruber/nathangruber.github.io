Search, Plan, Remember, and Share your HikesClick to View Live Site

Built on the LAMP stack, the application uses a MySQL database to manage users, trail locations, weather forecasts, trail journals, dates, and photos. It's useful to hikers who'd like to plan a hike with a weather forecast available, journal your hike, take photos, and share those photos on social media or hikeMe's trail search.

API's Used:

  • OpenWeatherMap.org
  • trailapi.com
