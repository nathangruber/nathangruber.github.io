hikeMe Search, Plan, Remember, and Share your Hikes

Project Description:

Built on the LAMP stack, the application uses a MySQL database to manage users, trail locations, weather information, trail journals, dates, and photos. It's useful to hikers who'd like to look for a hiking trail, plan a hike with a weather forecast, journal your hike, take photos, and share those photos if the user would like.

API's Used:

  • OpenWeatherMap.org
  • trailapi.com
